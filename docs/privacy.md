# Privacy Policy for The Climate Note

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

## Introduction

The Climate Note ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Used for account creation and authentication
- **Display Name**: Optional name shown to other users in community features
- **User-Generated Content**: Your personal climate action notes and goals
- **Activity Data**: Your daily reading streaks, goal progress, and impact classifications

### Information We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, precise location, contacts, photos, or crash/diagnostic data. We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app and your account
- Display today's climate article and personalised content
- Enable community features (shared action notes, leaderboard)
- Schedule optional local reminders on your device (no data leaves your device for this)
- Improve app features and security

**Local notifications**: Reminders are scheduled entirely on your device by iOS. We never transmit your notification preferences to our servers.

## AI Features

When you use AI-powered features (article insights, action suggestions, impact classification), the relevant text is sent to our server and processed by **Google Gemini** (Google LLC). This text is used solely to generate the response and is not retained by us or used to train AI models. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:
- **Community Features**: Your display name and action notes are visible to other users only when you explicitly choose to share them. Your email is never shown publicly.
- **Service Providers**: We use **Supabase** (secure cloud database and authentication) to store and manage your data. See [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- **AI Processing**: Text you submit for AI features is processed by Google Gemini as described above.
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

## Data Security

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure database storage with Supabase, including row-level security
- No advertising networks or third-party tracking

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- **Delete your account and all associated data** — available directly in the app: Profile → Delete account
- Opt out of local reminders at any time in the app or iOS Settings

To exercise any other rights, contact us at **privacy@theclimatenote.app**

## Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your personal data — notes, goals, streak history, and profile — is permanently deleted from our servers.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
